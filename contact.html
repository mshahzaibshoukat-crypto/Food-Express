Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 30 minutes. During busy hours it may take up to 45 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and cash on delivery.

Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, the minimum order for delivery is $15. Orders over $50 get free delivery!

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Absolutely! We have a wide range of vegetarian and vegan dishes clearly labelled on our menu.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 5 minutes of placing them. Call us immediately if you need to make changes.
